Understanding DPI (Dots Per Inch)

DPI determines the quality and size of your converted images:

  • 72 DPI: Standard for web viewing, smallest file size
  • 150 DPI: Good balance between quality and size
  • 300 DPI: Professional print quality
  • 600 DPI: High-detail scanning and archival

Higher DPI means better quality but larger file sizes. Choose based on your needs:

  • Web/Email: 72-150 DPI
  • Printing: 300 DPI
  • Detailed Archives: 600 DPI
